GDPR

Our clientele is based in multiple jurisdictions worldwide. To ensure flawless services, we have adopted the GDPR requirements to comply with the changes and provide a better user experience. We believe these new rules are better for our community.

To keep our customers' interests safe, we give our customers the right to request access to their personal information and get a copy of it. To know what information we hold, you can simply email us at info@fbspl.com . We will send you a copy of the details (Name, email, Phone, company name, Title, etc.) free of cost.

You can anytime request us to correct any incomplete or missing or may request to update the information we have for you by sending an email to info@fbspl.com with the relevant information. However, at our discretion, we may delete, correct, or remove incorrect data anytime for a better user experience.

As per our Privacy Policy, once the client ends the services, we remove all the relevant data shared by the client unless there is no overriding legitimate interest. The only data we hold will be the client's Company name and contact details for future reference. Such withdrawal of consent will be made by sending an email to info@fbspl.com with your relevant details.

All our users have the right to demand personal information in a commonly used, structured e-format anytime. You can make such requests by contacting the assigned Account Manager or emailing info@fbspl.com.

We will retain your personal information as long as it is necessary to carry out its processing activities during the contract period. We follow the retention policy of removing personal details 30 days after the last service date.

Please note that FBSPL will only keep your information for the duration mentioned above to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreement.

However, some of the information gathered about you may come from public sources on the web, such as other platforms. This information could also be collected by our partners or other third parties.

Thus, we suggest you review the information about you that is publicly available on other platforms and ensure only the details you have chosen are visible.
